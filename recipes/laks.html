Salmon

Ingredients:

  • Salmon filet
  • Minced Garlic
  • Honey
  • Apple cider vinegar or lemon juice.
  • Salt and pepper.

Steps:

  1. First, season the salmon fillets with salt, pepper and cayenne pepper.
  2. Make the honey garlic sauce by mixing the ingredients together.
  3. Heating up a skillet or pan and cook the salmon with garlic and the sauce.
  4. Add an extra step of broiling the salmon fillets in the oven.
